About Us

Miku e-SKpo is the music concert established in December 2020 and based on Jakarta, Indonesia. This concert established from collaboration of Crypton Company with Bluejacks. Our team consists of professional event-organizer, sound & lightning professionals, and stage planner and decor. This three-day festival upholds one of the most consequential and aspiring events in Indonesia.

A wide spectrum of sub-genres music category are given a platform through the multiple stages in the festival. We always push our limits to create an interesting, futuristic, and fun concerts which is hard to find on the other concert.
